About 3D Origami

There are different branches of Origami, 3D origami is the one that stands out for me because in the end, it does not look like the whole creation is made out of paper. 3D origami consists of different pieces of paper that are each folded the same way. In the end all the pieces are put together (most of the time without glue) into one.
On the beatiful background you can see "regular" origami. Below you will find 3 tutorials that I have followed, after going through the process of folding the pieces, you will be able to create something beautiful yourself! each tutorial is represented by clickable pandas.
